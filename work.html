Frequently Asked Questions

How do you select communities for your projects?

We conduct comprehensive needs assessments, considering factors like population size, existing infrastructure, community readiness, and potential for sustainable impact. We also prioritize communities that actively engage with our team.

How can communities apply for project support?

Communities can reach out through our contact channels with a detailed proposal outlining their needs, existing resources, and commitment to project sustainability. We review all applications and conduct site visits for promising proposals.

What is the typical duration of your projects?

Project duration varies based on scope and complexity. Education programs typically run 1-3 years, infrastructure projects 6-18 months, and capacity building initiatives 2-5 years. We focus on creating lasting impact rather than quick fixes.

How do you ensure project sustainability?

We build local capacity, train community members to manage projects independently, establish maintenance protocols, and create revenue streams where possible. We also maintain long-term relationships for ongoing support and monitoring.
