Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our office directly.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome during office hours, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure someone is available to meet with you and give you their full attention.

Do you accept international volunteers?

Yes, we welcome volunteers from around the world. International volunteers should contact us at least 3 months in advance to discuss visa requirements and program arrangements.

How can media representatives contact you?

Media inquiries should be directed to our communications team at media@aritfoundation.org or by selecting "Media Inquiry" in the contact form above.
