Frequently Asked Questions

How do you decide which focus areas to prioritize in different communities?

We conduct comprehensive community needs assessments, working directly with local leaders and residents to identify the most pressing challenges and opportunities for sustainable development.

Can communities request specific programs or interventions?

Absolutely! We encourage communities to reach out with their specific needs and ideas. We evaluate all requests based on our capacity, alignment with our mission, and potential for sustainable impact.

How do you ensure the sustainability of your programs?

We focus on building local capacity, training community members to continue programs independently, and creating revenue-generating activities that can fund ongoing operations.

Do you collaborate with other organizations in these focus areas?

Yes, we actively seek partnerships with government agencies, other NGOs, private sector organizations, and international development partners to maximize our impact and avoid duplication of efforts.
