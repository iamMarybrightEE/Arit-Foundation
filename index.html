Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main focus of Arit Foundation?

Our main focus is improving the quality of life for individuals and communities in Oro and Akwa Ibom state through education, skill development, financial investments, and community development programs.

How can I get involved with the foundation?

You can get involved by volunteering, making donations, partnering with us on projects, or participating in our community programs. Contact us for more information on how to contribute.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Oro community and the broader Akwa Ibom state, focusing on areas where we can make the most significant impact on people's lives.

How do you ensure transparency in your programs?

We maintain transparency through regular reporting, community feedback sessions, and data-backed approaches to all our initiatives. Our impact reports are available to the public.
